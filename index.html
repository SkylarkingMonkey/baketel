Imbiblia

imbiblia.com

Imbiblia is a cocktail recipe app, launched August 2021. It is a tool for bartenders, restaurants and drink nerds to quickly view drink recipes with a unique visual format. It features instant conversion, a batch calculator and in-depth historical analysis. Imbiblia hit #1 on the App Store in the United States and Canada, and has been featured in multiple publications. This app is built using Javascript/Preact, Python/Django and SQL. Everything from idea to design to coding to hosting was executed by yours truly.

Good Pizza

goodpizzayvr.com

Good Pizza is a reservation system built for a local start-up business in Vancouver, B.C. The system opens on Mondays at 9 am and logs reservations until options are sold out. Each reservation is logged on a custom admin page which also delivers a work list for pizza production. Each reservation also activates an email confirmation to be sent. Aesthetics were at the direction of the people of Good Pizza. Graphic design was done by a third party. Everything else including hosting was done by yours truly.
